Building open-source infrastructure for women's health research and privacy-first technology.

---

## The Problem

Critical data and tools surrounding women's health have been historically under-researched or locked inside proprietary silos. The code powering health technology shouldn't be a secret - and access to it shouldn't be a luxury.

I'm working to change that.

---

## What I Build

| Focus Area | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **Neurocognition & Hormonal Health** | Tools that track, analyze, and demystify how hormonal changes impact brain health |
| **Privacy-First AI** | Local, secure AI architectures for health data analysis - no black-box cloud models |
| **Secure Web Infrastructure** | Transparent web tools with strict API management and a no-compromises approach to data security |

---

## Why Open Source?

Closing the women's health research gap requires collaboration. By keeping this work open, I invite developers, researchers, and advocates to audit, improve, and deploy these tools globally.

If any of that resonates - whether you care about privacy, health equity, or just building things that matter - you're in the right place.

## Active Projects

**[selene](https://github.com/innacampo/selene)** - Privacy-first menopause assistant: local symptom tracking, RAG-backed chat, and clinician-style summaries powered by MedGemma. `Python`

**[clara](https://github.com/innacampo/clara)** - A multimodal AI safety net for clinical reasoning, detecting diagnostic shadowing before it harms patient care. `TypeScript`

**[align](https://github.com/innacampo/align)** - Translates moments of physiological disruption into professional clarity. `TypeScript`

**[lucia_agent](https://github.com/innacampo/lucia_agent)** - AI-powered scientific instrument that analyzes medical research to surface and mitigate diagnostic bias in women's health. Built with Google ADK & Gemini. `Python`
